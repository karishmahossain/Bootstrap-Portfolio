About Me

Karishma is an 8th Grade math teacher at KIPP Academy of Innovation. She served as a Teach for America Corps Member in the Las Vegas Valley and taught math at Eldorado High School for the past four years. In addition to teaching various content areas, Karishma worked closely with Communities In Schools to mentor at risk seniors and guided them towards graduation and post secondary opportunities, served as a Student Council advisor, and coached the Freshman Girls Basketball team. She earned an M.Ed. in Curriculum and Instruction from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas in 2016. Karishma also earned a B.B.A. in Management Information Systems from The University of Texas at Austin in 2013 and worked as a management consultant prior to her work in education. Outside of teaching, Karishma enjoys hiking, reading, and spending time with family.
